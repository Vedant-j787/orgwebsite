Who We Are
A technology company
rooted in craft

Nadaka Technologies is an independent technology company headquartered in Nagpur, India. We design and build digital products that sit at the intersection of intelligent software and thoughtful user experience.

Founded in 2024, we have grown from a small team of engineers with a shared vision into a focused studio of builders, designers, and strategists — all united by the belief that technology should feel human.

We work across consumer applications, developer tooling, and emerging platforms — always asking how technology can serve people better, not just faster.

Our Approach
We build slowly and intentionally. Every product we ship has been debated, refined, and tested until it feels inevitable — not just functional.
Our Scale
We are a small team by design. Staying lean lets us move thoughtfully, maintain quality, and genuinely care about every decision we make.
Our Home
Built in Nagpur, thinking globally. We believe world-class technology companies can be built anywhere — and we are proving it.
Our Mission
"To build technology that earns trust — through design that respects the user, and engineering that never cuts corners."

At Nadaka, we do not chase trends. We identify problems worth solving, assemble the right people to solve them, and build tools that outlast the moment they were made in. We measure success not by growth metrics alone, but by how meaningfully our products improve the lives of the people who use them.
